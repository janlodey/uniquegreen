We invest time, capital and expertise to grow sustainable businesses

Unique Green was created to make investments in early-stage technologies and ideas in the sustainable economy.

We are investing in early-stage innovative technology companies developing products and solutions for a more efficient and sustainable use of global resources.

The sectors that interest Unique Green are, among others, green energy generation, energy efficiency, energy storage, clean water, sustainable transport and smart cities.

We are interested in any early-stage business that aims to make a significant contribution in the fight against climate heating.

In return we provide strategic planning, business management and cash equity to startup and very early stage companies.

Our basic selection criteria for suitable sustainable startups are simple.

We want to work with teams that blend experience, expertise, and the right mindset, to develop innovative sustainable and profitable products and services that will make the world a better place.

What’s next?

Unique Green is usually the first equity investors in sustainable startup businesses and we generally act as lead investors. We offer companies support before, during, and after the investment.

Our first priority is to help our companies grow. We have many years’ experience of working in the international energy and environmental technology sectors so can provide a huge fund of experience, expertise and contacts to help cultivate our portfolio companies.

Unique Green does this by helping to develop relevant strategies and ensuring scalability for sustainable growth and international expansion.

We’re looking for exceptional companies that have set out to change the world and become market leaders.
